Breakfast Sandwich

Breakfast of champions

All of the things you want in the morning, in one breakfast sandwich

Indredients

  • Bacon
  • burger bun
  • eggs
  • cheese
  • Ketchup & Mayo

Method

  1. Toast the bun on a grill or in a pan with a little bit of oil
  2. Put the bacon on the griddle until it's nice and golden, or fry it again with a little oil, and if you want possible some honey
  3. On the non-stick grill put your eggs and cooked them sunny side up.
  4. Once everything is cooked layer it so it's bun, bacon, and slice of thing cheese, egg and then if needed a bit of ketchup or mayo.
  5. Enjoy your Breakfast!
