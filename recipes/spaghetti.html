Spaghetti Spectacular

Home made (with a little bit of sauce help) Spaghetti bolognese which is fast, takes less than 20 minutes in total and tastes great!

Indredients

  • Mince Beef 250g
  • Lloyd Grossman Spaghetti Bolognese Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti

Method

  1. Boil the kettle for the pasta
  2. Heat Pan to a a hot heat to cook the mince within 2-3 minutes without destroying the flavour
  3. Season a reasonable amount of salt and pepper, remember it will lose a lot of flavour whilst cooking fast
  4. After 2/3 of each piece of mince looks cooked then put your sauce in
  5. Turn the heat down to a low/medium temperature and slowly stir it
  6. Whilst thats cooking put your boiled water into the pan and put a handful of salt into the pan
  7. Once your pasta has cooked for 8 minutes or so in hot boiling water it should be fully cooked, It will continue to cook when out of the pan
  8. Let it cool down a little then put on some olive oil to keep it from sticking together
  9. And now serve the pasta on a plate and the bolognese on top.
